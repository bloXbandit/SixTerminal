# PTO Policy — Stelic Construction (DUMMY DATA)

**Document:** HR-POL-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Owner:** Human Resources

---

## PTO Accrual Schedule

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Days |
|---|---|
| 0–1 year | 10 days |
| 1–2 years | 12 days |
| 2–5 years | 15 days |
| 5–10 years | 18 days |
| 10+ years | 22 days |

## Carryover Policy
- Maximum of 5 unused PTO days may be carried into the next calendar year.
- Unused days beyond 5 are forfeited on December 31.

## PTO Request Process
1. Submit request in ADP at least 5 business days in advance for planned time off.
2. Manager approval required within 2 business days.
3. Emergency/unplanned absences: notify manager by 8:00 AM on the day of absence.

## Sick Leave
- Separate from PTO: 5 sick days per year, non-accruing, non-transferable.
- Doctor's note required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Holidays
- 10 company-observed holidays per year (see HR-POL-002 for full list).
